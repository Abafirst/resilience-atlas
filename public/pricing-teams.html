Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period.

What happens to my data if I downgrade?

Your data is never deleted when you downgrade. You'll retain access to your historical assessment results, though some advanced features may become inaccessible until you upgrade again.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial for Teams Starter and Teams Pro. No credit card required to start. Enterprise trials are arranged through our sales team.

How do you count "team members"?

A team member is any person who has completed an assessment linked to your organization. Invited members who haven't yet completed the assessment don't count toward your limit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Enterprise customers can also pay by invoice (bank transfer).

Is our assessment data private and secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Individual member scores are never shared with other organizations. Aggregated team reports anonymise individual responses. We are GDPR-compliant.

Can we migrate from the Business tier?

Existing Business tier customers are automatically migrated to Teams Pro with no disruption. You'll gain access to all new Pro features automatically. Contact support if you have questions.
