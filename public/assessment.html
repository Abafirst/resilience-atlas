What We Measure

The Six Dimensions of Resilience

Each dimension reflects a distinct capacity that contributes to how you navigate adversity. The assessment measures all six.

🤝

Relational

Your capacity to access and draw on support from others, maintain connections, and seek help when facing adversity.

🧠

Cognitive

How you interpret and reframe challenges. Your ability to shift perspective, find meaning, and construct narratives of growth.

💚

Somatic

How your body regulates stress. Your physiological capacity to move through tension, restore calm, and sustain energy.

💕

Emotional

Your capacity to acknowledge, experience, and move through challenging emotional states without becoming overwhelmed.

🌿

Spiritual

How meaning and purpose shape your resilience. Your connection to values and beliefs that sustain you through difficulty.

⚡

Agentic

Your capacity to take action in adversity, make decisions, and assert agency even in uncertainty.

The six dimensions are grounded in doctoral research across positive psychology, resilience science, and behavioral analysis.
