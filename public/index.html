Six Dimensions of Resilience

The assessment measures resilience across six evidence-based domains.

◈ Adaptive Resilience

The ability to adjust, pivot, and thrive amid change and uncertainty.

◈ Relational Resilience

Drawing strength from and contributing to the people around you.

◈ Existential Resilience

Finding meaning and purpose that sustains you through hardship.

◈ Emotional Resilience

Recognising, regulating, and recovering from intense emotions.

◈ Physical Resilience

Maintaining the body as a foundation for meeting life's demands.

◈ Cognitive Resilience

Thinking flexibly, challenging unhelpful patterns, and problem-solving effectively.

About Dr. Janeen Molchany

Janeen Molchany, Ph.D., BCBA

Dr. Molchany is a board-certified behavior analyst and researcher whose work focuses on human resilience, behavioral health, and evidence-based approaches to well-being. With decades of clinical and consulting experience, she developed the Resilience Assessment to give individuals an accessible, research-grounded window into their own adaptive capacity.

The assessment framework draws on the latest findings from positive psychology, behavioral science, and trauma-informed practice — presented in a format that is warm, accessible, and immediately actionable.
