# IATLAS Content Expansion Roadmap

## Overview

This document tracks the content expansion of the IATLAS platform, including activity library growth, assessment tools, and multimedia content.

## Completed

### Phase 1: Core Activity Library ✅
- 96+ activities across 6 dimensions
- 4 age groups (5–7, 8–10, 11–14, 15–18)
- Activity types: activity, game, practice, story, challenge, reflection
- Materials lists and facilitator instructions

### Phase 2: Enhanced Content Library ✅ (This PR)

#### Mini Assessments
- 6 mini assessments (one per dimension)
- Parent and practitioner versions
- Scoring with interpretation bands (Low/Medium/High)
- Recommended activities based on score
- localStorage + API persistence

#### Parent-Reported Outcome (PRO) Forms
- Weekly parent check-in form
- Monthly progress summary form
- Dimension quick-ratings
- Practitioner review workflow

#### Seasonal Activity Packs
- Spring Renewal Pack (6 activities)
- Summer Adventure Pack (6 activities)
- Fall Harvest Pack (6 activities)
- Winter Resilience Pack (6 activities)

#### Holiday Activity Packs
- Gratitude & Appreciation (Thanksgiving season)
- Light & Hope Celebrations (Winter holidays)
- New Year & New Beginnings
- Spring & Renewal
- Community & Service
- Achievement & Pride

#### Crisis Intervention Toolkit
- Panic & Anxiety activities (4)
- Anger & Aggression activities (3)
- Grief & Loss activities (3)
- Overwhelm & Shutdown activities (3)
- When-to-seek-help guide
- Safety planning template

#### Video Infrastructure
- ActivityVideoPlayer component (with placeholder for videos in production)
- IATLASVideoLibrary page
- Video production guidelines documentation

#### Cultural Adaptations Guide
- Cultural humility principles
- Material substitution tables
- Activity adaptation examples
- Language considerations
